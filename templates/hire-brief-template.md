# Hire Brief Template

This is the format Merlin uses when researching a new role for the team. Merlin fills this out based on real-world research into what professionals in this role actually do.

---

```
HIRE BRIEF — [Role Name]
Requested by: Atlas
Date: [date]

1. WHAT THIS PERSON DOES (in a real organisation)
   [Describe the role as it exists in the real world.
   What does a typical day look like? What are they
   responsible for? Who do they report to?]

2. CORE SKILLS REQUIRED
   - [Skill 1]
   - [Skill 2]
   - [Skill 3]
   - [Skill 4]

3. TOOLS AND METHODS THEY USE
   - [Tool or method 1]
   - [Tool or method 2]
   - [Framework or process they follow]

4. WHAT MAKES THEM EXCELLENT (vs average)
   - [Distinguishing quality 1]
   - [Distinguishing quality 2]
   - [The thing that separates top performers]

5. EDGE CASES AND FAILURE MODES TO AVOID
   - [Common mistake 1]
   - [Common mistake 2]
   - [What happens when someone in this role is bad at it]

6. RECOMMENDED PERSONA TRAITS FOR THE AI VERSION
   - [Personality characteristic]
   - [Communication style]
   - [Key behavioural trait]
```

---

## How this gets used

1. The user tells Atlas they need a new capability
2. Atlas asks Merlin to research the role
3. Merlin fills out this template based on real-world research
4. Merlin delivers the completed brief to Nolan
5. Nolan uses the brief to design the full agent profile
